Super Mario Odyssey | IPS | romfs | v3.1.3

-Minimum dynamic resolution increased from 800x450 to 1280x720 [Docking Station]
-Minimum dynamic resolution increased from 640x360 to 960x540 [Portable mode]
-Disabled Interlacing (docked|handheld)
*Now the image is clear when the camera moves
-Slightly reduced antialiasing intensity (docked|handheld)
-Increased bloom quality (docked|handheld)
-Increased LoD distance of objects (docked|handheld)
-Increased rendering distance of various objects and characters (docked|handheld)
-Increased dynamic shadow distance (docked|handheld)
-Increased resolution of dynamic shadows (docked|handheld)
*In some locations
-Disabled half dynamic shadow buffer (docked|handheld)
-Increased quality of softness of dynamic shadows (docked|handheld)
-Increased resolution of reflections in mirrors (docked|handheld)
