Darksiders III | IPS | romfs | v1.0.1

*a necessary measure to smooth out artifacts on the character due to TAA antialiasing

-Bloom quality increased (docked|handheld)

-Anisotropic filtering of world textures increased to 2x (docked|handheld)
-SSR Reflections Enabled (docked|handheld)
